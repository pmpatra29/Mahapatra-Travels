Our Listed Places

Lakshadweep, Union territory of India


Settled off the Kerala coast on Laccadive sea, Lakshadweep translates to ‘a hundred thousand islands’. A club of 36 atolls and coral reefs, is known for preserved ecology and water sports.

Taj Mahal, Agra, Uttar Pradesh


The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. Taj Mahal is listed as one of the modern seven wonders of the world.

Munnar, Town in Kerala


it's surrounded by rolling hills dotted with tea plantations. Eravikulam National Park, is home to the Lakkam Waterfalls, hiking trails and 2,695m-tall Anamudi Peak.

Hawa Mahal, Jaipur


This five-storey building has been built without a foundation and is known for its exceptional ventilation, which is also the reason behind its name, Hawa Mahal.
